Our Aim

At Akros we aim to work with you to create a complete online presence. We want to help you to build a strong online brand whether you are just starting out or running an existing website which you believe could be better.

Our Process

The first stage of the process is to understand your company and its aims. We want to know about your customers, your products and your project goals, so we can build an optimal solution for your requirements. If you have visited any websites which you like, do let us know and we can include similar ideas in your design. After discussing your content, colour palette and other design elements, we then create a set of prototypes of how your site could look. Once you are 100% happy with the design the team will then start to build the code. We aim to keep in close contact throughout the project duration, responding rapidly to any queries or suggestions you may have. If you request a content management system this can be built in either Wordpress or Perch.

Our Result

As a result of working with us you will have a fully responsive website that will be clean, modern, easy to use and tailored to your company mission. Your new website will be optimised for search engine use and will make an original statement on the web.
